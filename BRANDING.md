# Lamma+ Branding Guidelines

## Core Identity

**Name:** LAMMA+ (The Gathering)
**Symbol:** Palm Tree integrated with the "L" - representing shelter, sustenance, and gathering.
**Tagline:** Gather in Faith

## Logo Design

The LAMMA+ logo features a stylized palm tree that integrates with the letter "L":
- The palm tree trunk forms the vertical stroke of the "L"
- The horizontal stroke of the "L" extends from the base
- Palm fronds radiate elegantly from the top
- "AMMA" follows in bold sans-serif
- The "+" is displayed in gold/yellow (#F5B820)

### Logo Variants
- **Dark variant**: For dark/navy backgrounds - teal palm, white text, gold +
- **Light variant**: For light backgrounds - teal palm, dark text, gold +
- **Gold-bg variant**: For gold backgrounds - teal palm and text

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

- **Deep Teal (`#0D7377`)**: The palm tree color. Represents the Oasis, depth, and growth. Used for the logo icon, headers, and primary accents.
- **Warm Gold (`#F5B820`)**: The "+" sign color. Represents warmth, premium, and highlights. Used for primary actions, the + in LAMMA+, and active states.

### Secondary Colors

- **Teal Deep (`#1D4E5F`)**: Darker shade for gradients/borders.
- **Teal Light (`#E6F4F4`)**: Soft background for banners/cards (Light Mode).
- **Gold Light (`#FEF9E7`)**: Soft background for historical/special items.
- **Gold Dark (`#D4A01A`)**: Hover states for gold elements.

### Background Colors

- **Navy (`#0f172a`)**: Main background for Dark Mode (Slate 900).
- **Navy Card (`#1e293b`)**: Card/surfaces background in Dark Mode (Slate 800).
- **Navy Border (`#334155`)**: Borders in Dark Mode (Slate 700).
- **White/Off-white**: Clean surfaces for Light Mode.

## Typography

- **Logo Font**: Inter (Bold, -0.02em letter-spacing), all caps "LAMMA"
- **Headings**: Bold, Inter or system sans-serif
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif

## Icon Usage

- **Favicon**: Teal palm tree on transparent background
- **App Icon**: Teal palm tree on transparent background
- **Apple Touch Icon**: Gold palm tree on teal gradient background
- **OG Image**: Full branded logo on dark navy background

## Components

- **Buttons**:
  - **Primary**: Gold background (#F5B820), Dark text (High visibility)
  - **Secondary/Following**: Slate/Dark background, White text
  - **Ghost/Outline**: Border only
