## The Logo: The Gathering Tree

The Lamma+ tree is not just a logo - it's a symbol of what we're building.

### Symbolism

| Element | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **Roots** | Grounded in tradition, heritage, faith passed down through generations |
| **Trunk** | Strong foundation - the scholars, teachers, and educators who hold knowledge |
| **Branches** | Community spreading outward, reaching people across the world |
| **Leaves** | Individual seekers, each one growing, learning, flourishing |
| **The Whole Tree** | The **gathering place** - where people come together to learn and grow |

### Cultural Significance

| Culture | The Gathering Tree Tradition |
|---------|------------------------------|
| **African** | The village tree where elders teach, community decisions are made, stories are shared |
| **Islamic** | The Prophet ï·º often taught under trees; trees mentioned 26+ times in the Quran; Shajarat al-Ridwan (Tree of Allegiance) |
| **Somali** | Community gatherings under acacia trees for poetry, teaching, and counsel |
| **Arabic** | Majlis gatherings held under shade trees for learning and discussion |
| **Universal** | Every culture has the sacred tree where wisdom is shared |

### Why a Tree?

1. **Universal** - Trees are sacred across ALL faith traditions
2. **Inclusive** - Not tied to any single religion or culture
3. **Timeless** - Trees represent enduring wisdom
4. **Growing** - Always expanding, always alive
5. **Welcoming** - Shade is offered to everyone who seeks it
